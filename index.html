Yahtzee Game

Yahtzee Game-Desktop application developed with Java Swing (GUI)
The Yahtzee scorecard contains 13 different categories and in each round, after the third roll, the player must choose one of these categories. The score entered in the box depends on how well the five dice match the scoring rule for the category.

For-Tech

For-Tech-Web application developed with ASP.NET Core
This application offers two main features:

  1. Electronic Dictionary with technical terms

    2. Students and teachers can add terms to the dictionary, upvote or report existing terms and explanations. Users can search terms in Albanian and English. (Verbal search is available for English)


  2. Forum used by Engineering Students and community

    3. Users can make questions or open discussion related to engineering and science and receive answers from other students and teachers. They can comment on posts, upvote, mark as correct or report answers.


    Users are notified every time their posts are upvoted, reported or commented on. Depending on their contribution (positive/negative), they receive a certain amount of points.
